About PanIIT Rising Rajasthan — SAMBHAV

PanIIT and Rising Rajasthan have come together to create SAMBHAV—a powerful platform where ignited minds, innovators, and leaders unite to shape the future.

Sambhav is designed to be more than an event. It is a confluence of ideas, opportunities, and collaborations—bringing together entrepreneurs, corporates, policymakers, investors, Government, students, and the global IIT alumni network on one stage.

The platform is built to:

  • Inspire Dreamers – to transform bold visions into real-world impact.
  • Empower Builders – to showcase innovation across technology, infrastructure, sustainability, and social impact.
  • Connect Ecosystems – enabling meaningful interactions between startups, corporates, investors, and academia.
  • Celebrate Rajasthan & Beyond – highlighting the state’s potential while opening pathways for global partnerships.

By combining the strength of PanIIT’s global alumni network with Rajasthan’s energy and opportunity, SAMBHAV emerges as a movement for sustainable progress—local in spirit, global in reach, and lasting in impact. Here, Ideas ignite, Partnerships form, and the Roadmap to tomorrow begins.
